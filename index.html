THE BLOG

Start With What You Can't Not Do

19 May 2025

Every story starts somewhere. Mine did in Beirut — a lively city that pulses with contradiction and creativity — and with a restless instinct that kept pulling me toward meaning, even when the path wasn’t clear.

I’ve moved between worlds: from corporate campaigns to grassroots projects, from NGO strategy sessions to nights spent writing film scripts. I’ve worked as a digital media strategist, brand consultant, filmmaker, and content producer. Along the way, I’ve helped brands articulate what they stand for and crafted narratives that do more than sell — they resonate, they build trust, and they last.

This website is a home for that kind of work.

It’s where strategy meets story. Where brands find clarity. And where I share not just the outcomes — but the thought behind them. The questions I ask. The tension between intuition and precision. The belief that creativity is not decoration; it’s direction.

Whether you're here looking for someone to help you sharpen your brand, craft a compelling message, or build content that connects — this is the place. And if you’re simply curious about how ideas take shape, how a story becomes a strategy, or how personal politics can live inside a professional framework, I hope you find something here that stays with you.

This is only the beginning.

Let’s see where it goes.

About Me

I’ve always been captivated by the power of storytelling—how it can transform ideas into emotions, brands into movements, and audiences into communities.

Over the past 10 years, I’ve dedicated my career to helping brands restructure their narratives, ensuring they not only stand out but also connect deeply with their communities. My work revolves around turning complex concepts into clear, compelling stories that drive engagement, build trust, and inspire action
